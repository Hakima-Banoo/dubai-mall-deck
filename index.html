01 — The Destination

More Than a Mall.
A Global Icon.

Inaugurated in 2008 and operated by Emaar Properties, Dubai Mall anchors Downtown Dubai — steps from Burj Khalifa and the world's largest choreographed fountain. For two consecutive years it has held the title of Most Visited Place on Earth.

  • Galeries Lafayette & Bloomingdale's as anchor department stores
  • Fashion Avenue — 150+ luxury houses: Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Hermès, Rolex
  • Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo — world's largest acrylic viewing panel
  • Olympic-sized Ice Rink, KidZania, 22-screen Reel Cinemas, Play DXB
  • Metro link, 14,000 parking bays, connected 5-star hotels & residences
  • AED 1.5B expansion underway — 240 new luxury & F&B outlets by 2026
